## Chappter is your life, written as a story.

Every day, you live moments worth remembering — small or big, ordinary or unforgettable.
Chappter helps you capture them effortlessly and turns them into a structured, meaningful narrative.

### Your life, one chapter at a time

Instead of asking you to write everything manually, Chappter builds your story for you.

It automatically organizes your days into chapters — like a book:

- A trip becomes a chapter
- A new job becomes a chapter
- Even a simple week can become a story worth keeping

You don’t need to think about structure. It’s already there.

### Effortless journaling

Chappter connects to the tools you already use:

- your calendar
- your photos
- your daily activity

From these, it creates a timeline of your life and suggests beautifully written summaries.

You can:

- keep it as is
- edit it
- or make it completely your own

No pressure. Just a starting point.

### More than memories

Chappter doesn’t just store what happened — it helps you understand it.

It highlights:

- important moments
- personal milestones
- patterns in your life

Over time, you don’t just remember your past — you see how your story evolves.

### A new way to look back

With Chappter, you can:

- revisit a specific day
- explore an entire chapter
- or scroll through your life timeline

It’s like flipping through a book — except it’s yours.
